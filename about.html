Our
Story

Est. 1998

We Build More
Than Structures

Stackly Construction was born from a simple belief: every building should tell a story of human ambition, precision, and care. Founded in 1998 in Dubai, we set out to redefine construction excellence on a global scale.

Over 28 years, we have delivered more than 250 landmark projects across 40+ countries, partnering with governments, developers, and architects to shape some of the world's most recognisable skylines.

Today, Stackly is synonymous with reliability, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to sustainability — a promise to our clients, the communities we build in, and the planet we share.
